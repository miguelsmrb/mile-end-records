About the Shop

Mile End Records is a Montreal boutique focused on alternative and dance music, plus a carefully curated selection of turntables, mixers, cartridges, and studio accessories. The shop blends community events with a modern online storefront.

Store Focus

  • Alternative and indie discoveries with new pressings every week.
  • Dance and electronic essentials from house, techno, and ambient labels.
  • Music equipment and gear: turntables, headphones, monitors, and accessories.
  • Local Montreal artists, pop-ups, and listening sessions.
